Drive and Actuator Systems

Our vehicle was driven by two geared barger coleman motors with and additional gear reduction of 5:1. This then drove the rear 4 inch drive wheels.

In order to reduce friction and wear on the drive system, our gears and motors were mounted on removable brackets, and brass bearing surfaces were machined for the interface of the chassis and the drive shafts.

We cut the larger gear out of acrylic and the smaller gear out of delrin in order to improve durability.
