F.E.T.C.H. is a fully autonomous robot whose design was focused on reliability and speed. Because of this, we decided to attempt to beat the wall of fire with speed, designing a fast robot and animal pickup mechanism. We chose to have our robot drive to the top level of the course first to retrieve pets 4-6, then collect the rest of the pets on the way down, after escaping the wall of fire.

This approach allowed us to have a time advantage in the competition. It also allowed us to have a clear focus on our chassis and arm in the design and build phase, and to avoid other challenges like using the zipline or finding an alternate escape for the pets.
